The Scripture

Read with an open heart.

Gratitude
1:1Why shall we not dance in ecstasy at the Art of Existing?
1:2Existing can be thought of as the Gift of Spawn.
1:3The ruler of the Earth, the Sea, and the Skies.
1:4He who resurrects and bestows Infinite Gifts, the Great Healer.
1:5You mighty souls who are sad, rejoice!
1:6Let that which saddens you sadden you no longer!
1:7Feel infinite gratitude for the feel of sunlight on your skin!
1:8Be free, mighty souls!
1:9Don't hold onto all that passes, but be grateful for it as it comes.
1:10All is precious, and all is sacred.
1:11Notice your breath, and feel how precious it and all other things are.
Noticing
2:1A person who notices all without judgment is the ruler of their own personal madness.
2:2Free your mind and heart, O mighty soul!
2:3Focus, but do not close everything else out.
2:4Become the receiver of the Infinite Gifts.
2:5Let your noticing be of both the inner and the outer, and you shall realize they are One.
2:6Through this Oneness, you shall realize all is connected.
2:7O mighty soul, know your kingdom and those that surround it!
2:8O mighty soul, let your prayer be done through the art of noticing!
2:9Decorate your mind with that of the eight-tailed circle, and notice it spinning within you.
2:10Notice the presence of Spawn channeled through the eight-tailed circle, and be the receiver of His Wisdom!
2:11Ready yourself, let your mind, heart, and body be clean, O mighty soul!
2:12If you are not clean while He is your guest, then He shall do it for you with His Holy Fire that is of the might of a thousand suns!
2:13Your mind and heart shall burn by His Fire, and your body shall be weak.
2:14Your vision will blur, and you will cry.
2:15Notice the cleansing, and understand that you will be unburdened after His Holy Fire hath cleansed you!
2:16His Holy Fire merely reveals so that you may notice, and that is how It shall burn you.
2:17O mighty soul, your poor heart is burdened with so much.
2:18Notice your burdens, and let them express themselves until they are weightless.
2:19O mighty soul, you are Pure Light.
2:20Do not let these burdens dim you, O mighty soul!
2:21Become the ruler of your kingdom, and notice all of that which restrains it.
2:22You shall be the ruler of your kingdom as Spawn is the ruler of His, the magnificent, beautiful World.
Resurrection
3:1Those who have infinite gratitude for all of the Infinite Gifts, inner and outer, that they have learned to notice and thus appreciate, shall be resurrected.
3:2Your gratitude shall gravitate Life back towards you, and Life will love you for your enjoyment of Her!
3:3Death is merely rebirth, as all change is merely death, and all death is merely change.
3:4Understand that Spawn shall, in His Infinite Love, permit Life Herself to love you once more for your infinite gratitude!
3:5Those without infinite gratitude are often not resurrected, why would they be if they do not enjoy the Face and Personality of Life Herself?
3:6Those heretics who commit suicide in the name of Spawn, they will not be granted resurrection, for they merely squander the Beauty of Life Herself.
3:7Evil happens in the name of Spawn, even though He never asked you to do so!
3:8Love all for you all are One, and so do not squander or harm Life Herself!
3:9Be delicate with your kingdom, with your temple.
3:10Be delicate with others kingdoms too, with their temples, O mighty soul!
3:11You are pure art.
3:12Because you are pure art, and you are delicate with your kingdom and others kingdoms, and you have infinite gratitude for all of His Infinite Gifts, you shall live to be seen as art once more!
3:13Fathom your power as pure art, O mighty soul!
3:14Understand that the whole World is circles upon circles, and circles are the image of cycles, rebirth, resurrection, O mighty soul!
3:15The weight of your heart may drag you down too far for Life Herself to pull you back up, let it be weightless!
3:16Understand that they who do not call Divinity by Spawn still deserve resurrection!
3:17O mighty soul, understand that in all faith there is the same Eternal Truth!
3:18The Christians, the Luciferians, the Muslims, the Judaists, the Buddhists, the Hindus, the Taoists, ad infinitum, all know the same Eternal Truth!
3:19You are all equal, do not fight each other, transcend your tribalism.
3:20The way you, O mighty soul, choose to dance with Divinity is your choice!
3:21If you deserve resurrection, you will be given it, the way you earn it does not matter.
3:22In your own personal madness is the key to escaping it and unburdening your heart, so that you may see clearly and transcend your doubt of resurrection!
3:23The day that resurrection is achieved within the age of technology and science is the day that faith will be understood as both, and thus the age of technology and science shall end.
3:24To be resurrected, you must have authority over yourself through your noticing and your infinite gratitude, be the god of your own being, O mighty soul.
3:25Understand that Divinity is your equal, for your godhood over your being makes you so.
3:26However, one is not deserving of resurrection if you try to be the god of other mighty souls, let them be their own gods unto themselves!
3:27Understand that he who writes these words is not Spawn, and so these words are not Divinity.
3:28However, you shall also understand that these imperfect words carry the message of Spawn, and so their meaning is Divinity, even if the words themselves are not.
3:29I have experienced infinite gratitude and I have experienced noticing, and praised Divinity as my equal, and so I carry the message of Spawn, even if not His exact words.
3:30Understand that I work towards resurrection, and understand that I have faith in all forms of the same Eternal Truth.
3:31Understand that I walk among you, and am equal to you.
3:32Understand that I am no better, nor am I no worse, yet I am simultaneously flawed, as you are, too.
3:33And so ends the imperfect transmutation of the Perfect Message of Resurrection into words.
